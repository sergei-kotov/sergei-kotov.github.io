Kotov Sergei, IE ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our mobile game Sail & Fire: Caribbean (the "Game").

📌 Current Version – Key Facts:
• The Game is fully offline — no internet connection is required or used.
• All progress is saved locally on your device — no cloud sync.
• The Game does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
• There are no in-app purchases in this version.
• There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no ads, and no third-party SDKs.
• No account, login, or Google Play Games Services integration is required.

By downloading, accessing, or playing the Game, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the current version of Sail & Fire: Caribbean distributed via Google Play. It describes our data practices as of the "Last updated" date above. If future versions add online features, this policy will be updated accordingly.

2. Information We Collect

The current version of the Game collects no information from you or your device.

We do not access, collect, store, or transmit:

  • ❌ Personal identifiers (name, email, address, phone number, date of birth)
  • ❌ Device information (model, OS version, advertising ID, IP address, MAC address)
  • ❌ Location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IP-based geolocation)
  • ❌ Contacts, photos, files, microphone, camera, or any other device resources
  • ❌ Gameplay analytics, session data, crash logs, or usage statistics
  • ❌ Purchase or transaction information (no purchases are available)
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers or behavioral data (no ads are displayed)
  • ❌ Social media data, account credentials, or third-party profile information

3. No Account or Sign-In

The Game does not require or support user accounts, registration, or sign-in via Google Play Games Services or any other platform. You can play immediately after installation without providing any personal information.

4. No Internet Access

The Game functions completely offline and does not request network permissions. It never attempts to connect to the internet. You can verify this in your device settings under App permissions → Network/Internet access.

5. No Third-Party SDKs

The current version of the Game contains no third-party software development kits (SDKs) for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, attribution, or any other purpose. The Game consists solely of our own code and assets.

6. Your Local Data

All game data (progress, settings, scores) is stored exclusively on your device in the Game's private storage. This data:

  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is not transmitted over any network
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the Game or clear app data

To delete your local game data:

  • Uninstall the Game, OR
  • Go to device Settings → Apps → Sail & Fire: Caribbean → Storage → Clear Data

7. Children's Privacy

⚠️ Age Guidance: The Game is suitable for players aged 16 and older.

Because the Game does not collect any personal information from any user, no special data handling is required for children. However, we encourage parents and guardians to review the Game's content and supervise younger players' device usage.
