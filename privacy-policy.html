Kotov Sergei, IE ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game Sail & Fire: Caribbean (the "Game").

📌 Key points about this Game:
• The Game works primarily offline — internet is only needed for in-app purchases and analytics.
• Your game progress is saved locally on your device — there is no cloud save.
• When you download the Game from Google Play, it is automatically linked to your Google Play account via Google Play Games Services (GPGS). This allows us to process purchases and identify you for anti-fraud purposes.
• We do not receive your email address, name, or profile picture from Google.

By downloading, accessing, or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Game.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the Game:

1.1 Information from Google Play Games Services (GPGS)

The Game integrates Google Play Games Services for user identification and in-app purchases. When you launch the Game for the first time, Google Play automatically provides us with:

  • Game Player ID — a unique, anonymous identifier specific to this Game. It does not contain your name, email address, or any personal information. This ID is generated automatically by Google Play Games Services.
  • Purchase tokens — transaction identifiers for each in-app purchase you make, used to validate receipts.

Note for EU users: While the Game Player ID is pseudonymized and cannot directly identify you, it may be considered personal data under GDPR when combined with other technical information. We process it solely for purchase validation and fraud prevention, based on our legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

1.2 Automatic Linking to Google Play Account

  • When you download the Game from Google Play, it is automatically linked to your Google Play account through Google Play Games Services. This is required for in-app purchases to function properly.
  • No explicit "sign-in" action is required from you — the linking happens seamlessly and automatically when you launch the Game.
  • This automatic linking does not give us access to your Google account credentials, email address, contacts, or any personal data beyond the Game Player ID described above.

1.3 Information Collected Automatically (Anonymous)

  • Device and Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, language settings, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Usage Analytics (Firebase): We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect aggregated, anonymous data about gameplay (e.g., session duration, levels completed, feature usage). This data cannot be used to identify you personally — it only helps us understand how players interact with the Game so we can fix bugs and improve design.

1.4 Purchase Information (Google Play Billing)

  • All in-app purchases are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment card details, billing address, or any sensitive financial information. We only receive a transaction confirmation and purchase token from Google.

1.5 Information You Provide Directly

  • Support Communications: If you contact us for support via email, we will collect the information you voluntarily provide (e.g., description of the issue, screenshots, your email address for replies).

1.6 What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No email addresses, real names, or profile pictures (we do not receive these from Google)
  • ❌ No separate login or password — the Game uses automatic GPGS integration
  • ❌ No cloud save synchronization — all progress is stored locally on your device
  • ❌ No location data (GPS or IP-based tracking)
  • ❌ No contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • ❌ No biometric data
  • ❌ No advertising ID or behavioral profiling for ads (the Game has no third-party ads)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To associate in-app purchases with your Google Play account (so purchases are validated and fraud is prevented).
  • Important note about cross-device purchases: Because the Game does not use cloud saves, purchases made on one device cannot be automatically restored on another device. Each device maintains its own local game state.
  • To analyze Game performance, detect crashes, and improve stability (via Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics).
  • To respond to your support requests and provide customer service.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of the Game.

4.1 Deleting Your Data / Removing Google Play Association

Because the Game uses Google Play Games Services for automatic identification (not a separate login system), there is no traditional "account" to delete. However:

To remove the Game's access to your Google Play Games profile:
Open the Google Play Games app on your device → Settings → Turn off "Automatically sign in to supported games". Note that this will prevent future purchases and may cause the Game to lose purchase validation ability.

To delete your local game progress:
Your game progress is stored only on your device. To delete it:
• Uninstall the Game, OR
• Go to device Settings → Apps → Sail & Fire: Caribbean → Storage → Clear Data

To request deletion of any stored support data (e.g., email correspondence):
Contact us at sk374962610@gmail.com. We will delete any records associated with your request within 30 days.

To view or delete your Google Play purchase history:
Purchase receipts are stored by Google, not by us. You can manage your Google Play purchase history at play.google.com/store/account.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at sk374962610@gmail.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30 days).
