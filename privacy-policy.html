Privacy Policy for Sail & Fire: Caribbean

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Kotov Sergei, IE ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you play our mobile game Sail & Fire: Caribbean (the "Game").

📌 Key points about this Game:
• The Game is fully offline — no internet connection is required to play.
• Your game progress is saved locally on your device — there is no cloud save.
• The Game does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
• There are no in-app purchases in the current version.
• There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no ads of any kind.

1. Information We Collect

The Game collects no information from you or your device. Period.

We do not access, collect, store, or transmit:

  • ❌ Any personal identifiers (name, email, address, phone number)
  • ❌ Device information (model, OS version, identifiers)
  • ❌ Location data (GPS or IP address)
  • ❌ Contacts, photos, files, or any other data on your device
  • ❌ Gameplay analytics, crash logs, or usage statistics
  • ❌ Purchase information (there are no purchases)
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers (there are no ads)

2. No Account, No Sign-In

The Game does not require or support accounts, logins, or Google Play Games Services. You can play immediately after installation without any registration.

3. No Internet Access Required

The Game functions completely offline. It never attempts to connect to the internet. You can verify this by checking the Game's network permissions in your device settings.

4. No Third-Party SDKs

The Game contains no third-party software development kits (SDKs) for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any other purpose.

5. Your Local Game Progress

All game data (scores, progress, settings) is stored exclusively on your device. To delete this data:

  • Uninstall the Game, OR
  • Go to device Settings → Apps → Sail & Fire: Caribbean → Storage → Clear Data

6. Children's Privacy

The Game does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. No special accommodations are required because no data collection occurs.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in future versions of the Game. If we add features that involve data collection (e.g., in-app purchases, analytics), we will update this policy and notify users via the Game's description in Google Play or an in-app notice.
