Kotov Sergei, IE ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game Sail & Fire: Caribbean (the "Game").

📌 Key points about this Game:
• The Game works primarily offline — internet is only needed for in-app purchases and analytics.
• Your game progress is saved locally on your device — there is no cloud save.
• When you launch the Game, it automatically links to your Google Play account via Google Play Games Services (GPGS). This provides a unique Game Player ID for purchase validation and anti-fraud. We do not receive your email, name, or profile picture.
• Data collection does not start on install. Firebase Analytics initializes only after you reach the main menu or perform your first in-game action.

By downloading, accessing, or playing the Game, you acknowledge that we will process your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Game.

1. Scope & Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected when you use Sail & Fire: Caribbean on mobile devices distributed via Google Play. It does not address the privacy practices of third-party platforms (e.g., Google Play Store, Google Play Games) beyond the data they share directly with us for Game functionality.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the Game, validate purchases, and ensure stability.

2.1 When Data Collection Begins

️ Timing of Collection:
• The Game does not collect or transmit any data immediately upon installation or initial launch.
• Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics initialize only after you reach the main menu or interact with the Game.
• Google Play Billing activates only when you open the in-game store or attempt a purchase.
• You can play fully offline without triggering any data collection by disabling internet access before launching.

2.2 Information from Google Play Games Services (GPGS)

Upon first connection, GPGS automatically provides:

  • Game Player ID: A unique, pseudonymous identifier specific to this Game. It contains no email, name, or personal identifiers.
  • Purchase Tokens: Transaction receipts for in-app purchases, used solely for validation.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device & Technical Data: Device model, OS version, language settings, crash logs, and performance metrics.
  • Usage Analytics (Firebase): Aggregated, non-personally identifiable gameplay data (session duration, levels completed, feature usage) to improve stability and design.

2.4 Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Support Communications: If you email us, we collect your email address and the content of your message to provide assistance.

2.5 What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Email addresses, real names, or profile pictures from Google
  • ❌ Cloud save data or cross-device synchronization
  • ❌ Precise location data (GPS) or IP-based tracking
  • ❌ Contacts, photos, or device files
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers for targeted ads (the Game contains no third-party ads)
  • ❌ Biometric or sensitive personal data

3. Why We Collect Your Information

We process your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Operate the Game: Validate purchases, prevent fraud, and ensure core functionality.
  • Analytics & Stability: Analyze crash reports and usage patterns to fix bugs and improve performance.
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and resolve technical issues.
  • Legal Compliance: Maintain tax records for purchases and comply with applicable laws.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Google (Firebase, Play Games Services, Play Billing) under strict confidentiality and data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, safety, or integrity of the Game.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality obligations.

5. Your Choices

  • Opt-out of Analytics: Disable internet access for the Game in device settings. Offline play remains fully functional.
  • Manage GPGS Linking: Google Play Games app → Settings → Toggle off "Automatically sign in to supported games". Note: This may disable purchase validation.
  • Delete Local Progress: Uninstall the Game or go to Settings → Apps → Sail & Fire: Caribbean → Storage → Clear Data.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Since we do not store personal data on external servers (support emails are stored securely in our email provider, and analytics are handled by Google's encrypted infrastructure), the risk of breach is minimal. However, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to safeguard your device and Google account credentials.

8. Transfers of Your Information

As a globally distributed game, your information may be processed in countries outside your residence. Firebase and Google Play services operate on secure global infrastructure. For transfers from the EEA/UK/Switzerland, we rely on Google's compliance with the EU-US Data Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure adequate protection.

9. Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary:

  • Local Game Progress: Stored on your device until uninstall or manual data clearance.
  • Firebase Analytics: Retained by Google per their policy (typically 14 months for raw data, then aggregated).
  • Support Emails: Retained for up to 12 months after issue resolution, then securely deleted.
  • Purchase Tokens: Retained up to 3 years for fraud prevention and tax compliance.

10. Children

️ Age Restriction: The Game is not intended for children under the age of sixteen (16).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. Because GPGS provides only a pseudonymous Player ID, we cannot verify user age. If you believe a child under 16 has provided personal data (e.g., via support email), contact us immediately at sk374962610@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete it.
