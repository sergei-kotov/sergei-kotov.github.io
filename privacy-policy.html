Kotov Sergei, IE ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game Sail & Fire: Caribbean (the "Game").

Note: The Game is designed primarily for offline play. Optional online features (Google Sign-In, Firebase Analytics, in-app purchases) require internet connectivity and are governed by this Privacy Policy.

By downloading, accessing, or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Game.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the Game:

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Google Account Data: When you sign in via Google Play Games or Google Sign-In, we may receive your Google user ID, public profile name, and email address (subject to your Google privacy settings and permissions you grant).
  • Support Communications: If you contact us for support, we may collect the information you voluntarily provide (e.g., description of the issue, screenshots).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, language settings, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Usage Analytics (Firebase): We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect aggregated usage data and technical identifiers (such as advertising ID and app instance ID) to understand gameplay patterns. This data is not used to identify you personally and is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Purchase Information: All in-app purchases are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment card details, billing address, or other sensitive financial information. We receive only a transaction confirmation and purchase token from Google.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with your Google account, Google may share limited profile information with us as permitted by your account settings and Google's policies. You can manage permissions at any time via your Google Account settings: myaccount.google.com/permissions

1.4 What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No sensitive personal identifiers beyond what Google provides
  • ❌ No cloud save synchronization (game progress stored locally)
  • ❌ No precise location data (GPS)
  • ❌ No contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • ❌ No biometric data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To enable optional account synchronization and restore purchases across devices.
  • To process and deliver in-app purchases via Google Play.
  • To analyze Game performance, detect crashes, and improve stability (via Firebase).
  • To respond to your support requests and provide customer service.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of the Game.
  • To personalize your gaming experience and remember your preferences.

3.1 No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. This includes users in California and other jurisdictions with "sale" definitions under privacy laws.

3.3 Legal Requirements

If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ourselves, our users, or the public.

3.4 Business Transfers

In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you of any such change and ensure your data remains protected.

All third parties are required to handle your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.

4. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access & Correction: Request a copy of your data or ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data. Note: This may result in loss of your saved game progress.
  • Opt-out of Analytics: You can limit Firebase data collection by adjusting your device settings or disabling internet access for the Game (some features may be unavailable).
  • Marketing Communications: The Game does not send promotional emails. You may manage push notifications via your device settings.
  • Google Account Controls: You can manage permissions for Google Sign-In and review data shared with apps via your Google Account settings: myaccount.google.com
  • Manage Google Permissions: You can review and revoke app permissions at any time via your Google Account: myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Delete Game Progress: All your game data is saved locally on your device. To delete it, simply uninstall the Game or clear its app data via your device's Settings → Apps → Sail & Fire: Caribbean → Storage → Clear Data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at sk374962610@gmail.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to safeguard your Google account credentials and device.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide and maintain the Game;
  • Comply with legal obligations;
  • Resolve disputes; or
  • Enforce our agreements.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. Anonymous Firebase analytics data is retained by Google according to their retention policies (typically 14 months for raw data, then aggregated). No personal data is retained by us beyond what is necessary. Your local game progress stays on your device until you choose to uninstall or clear app data.

The Game is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at sk374962610@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete such information.

Google complies with international data transfer frameworks such as the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers, in compliance with applicable laws (e.g., GDPR).
