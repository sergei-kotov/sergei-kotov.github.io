We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the Game:

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with your Google account, Google may share limited profile information with us as permitted by your account settings and Google's policies.
