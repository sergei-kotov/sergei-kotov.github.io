Privacy Policy for Sail & Fire: Caribbean

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Kotov Sergei, IE ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our offline mobile game Sail & Fire: Caribbean (the "Game").

Important note: The Game is designed to work fully offline. We do not collect any personal data for cloud saves, account creation, or online leaderboards. All your game progress is stored exclusively on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Since the Game works offline without accounts or cloud features, we collect only minimal, non-personal technical data to improve stability and gameplay experience:

1.1 Automatically Collected (Anonymous Only)

  • Device and Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, language settings, and crash reports.
  • Usage Analytics (Firebase): We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect aggregated, anonymous data about gameplay (e.g., which levels are played, session duration, feature usage). This data cannot be used to identify you personally — it only helps us understand how players interact with the Game so we can fix bugs and improve design.

1.2 Purchase Information (Google Play Billing)

  • All in-app purchases (if any) are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment card details, billing addresses, or financial information. We only receive a confirmation from Google that a purchase was completed.

1.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No personal identifiers (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • ❌ No account creation or login credentials
  • ❌ No cloud save synchronization
  • ❌ No location data (GPS or IP-based)
  • ❌ No contacts, photos, or files from your device

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited anonymous data only for:

  • Analyzing crash reports to fix technical issues (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Understanding which game features are popular to guide future updates (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Processing purchase receipts (via Google Play) — note that your progress is never "synced" or "restored" because the Game has no cloud saves.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade any information. We may share anonymous, aggregated technical data only with:

  • Google (Firebase): For crash reporting and analytics. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law to comply with a legal process or government request.

4. Your Choices and Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data (names, emails, etc.), there is no "personal information" stored on our servers that could be accessed, corrected, or deleted.

  • Opt-out of Analytics: You can disable Firebase data collection by turning off internet access for the Game in your device settings (some features may still work offline).
  • Delete Game Progress: All your game data is saved locally on your device. To delete it, simply uninstall the Game or clear its app data via your device's Settings → Apps → Sail & Fire: Caribbean → Storage → Clear Data.
  • Withdraw Consent: By uninstalling the Game, you withdraw any consent for future anonymous analytics collection.

If you have any questions, contact us at sk374962610@gmail.com.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on any external servers (except anonymous Firebase crash logs which are encrypted in transit), the risk of your personal information being compromised is extremely low. The Game itself does not transmit any personal data over the internet except for crash reports and anonymous analytics.

6. Data Retention

Anonymous Firebase analytics data is retained by Google according to their retention policies (typically 14 months for raw data, then aggregated). No personal data is retained by us. Your local game progress stays on your device until you choose to uninstall or clear app data.

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because our data collection is completely anonymous and does not include names, emails, or identifiers, no special child-specific data handling is required. If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

8. International Data Transfers

Firebase services (Google) may process anonymous crash and analytics data on servers located in various countries. This data contains no personal identifiers. Google complies with international data transfer frameworks such as the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will change. For material changes, we will notify you via an in-game notice (if internet is available) or by updating this page. Your continued use of the Game constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
